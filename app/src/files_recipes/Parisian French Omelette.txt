Ingredients:
Eggs - 3
Milk - 2 tbsp
Butter - 1 tbsp
Grated Gruyere cheese - 1/4 cup
Tomatoes - 1 (sliced)
Green onions - 2 tbsp (chopped)
Salt, pepper - to taste


Instructions:
In a bowl, whisk together eggs and milk. Season with salt and pepper.
Heat butter in a non-stick pan over medium heat.
Pour the egg mixture into the pan and let it set slightly.
Sprinkle grated Gruyere cheese, tomatoes, and green onions over one half of the omelette.
Fold the other half over the filling and cook until the cheese melts.
Slide the omelette onto a plate, cut into wedges, and serve.
